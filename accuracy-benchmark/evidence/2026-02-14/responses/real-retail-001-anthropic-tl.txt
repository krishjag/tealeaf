**Key Findings:**
- Non-General Mills products have **15% more calories** on average
- Non-General Mills products have **43% MORE fiber**
- Non-General Mills products have **48% MORE protein**
- Non-General Mills products have **19% LESS sugar**
- This is heavily influenced by the Dragons Blend cereal (high protein/fiber, low sugar)

## Summary Insights:

1. **Most Nutritious Overall:** Dragons Blend - highest protein (21.4g), excellent fiber (14.3g), low sugar ratio (0.75), zero sodium
2. **Best for Sugar Control:** Cheerios varieties - sugar-to-fiber ratios under 0.5
3. **Most Fortified:** Total Cereal - 100% DV for most vitamins and iron
4. **Highest Sugar:** Cookie Crisp, Lucky Charms, Breakfast Pack (33.3g per 100g)
5. **Sodium Concern:** Rice Chex at 775mg is very high; 9 products exceed 500mg
6. **General Mills Pattern:** Higher sugar, lower fiber/protein than specialty brands
