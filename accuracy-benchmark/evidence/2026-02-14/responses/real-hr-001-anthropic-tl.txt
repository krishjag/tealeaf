## 7. Private Sector vs Government Comparison

| Metric | Total Private | Government | Difference |
|--------|---------------|------------|------------|
| **Average Hire Rate** | **3.68%** | **1.45%** | Private +2.23 pp |
| **Average Quit Rate** | **2.21%** | **0.80%** | Private +1.41 pp |
| **Average Layoff Rate** | **1.23%** | **0.33%** | Private +0.90 pp |

### Key Findings:
- **Private sector has substantially higher hire rates** (3.68% vs 1.45%) - 2.5x higher than government
- **Private sector has substantially higher quit rates** (2.21% vs 0.80%) - 2.8x higher than government
- **Private sector has substantially higher layoff rates** (1.23% vs 0.33%) - 3.7x higher than government

**Interpretation**: Government employment is significantly more stable with lower turnover in all dimensions. Government workers are less likely to quit, less likely to be laid off, and government hiring activity is much slower - consistent with government's hardest-to-fill positions (highest JO/HI ratio of 2.35).
