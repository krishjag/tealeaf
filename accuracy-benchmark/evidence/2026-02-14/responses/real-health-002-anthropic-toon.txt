## üîç Key Insights Summary

1. **Urban-Rural Divide**: The smallest, most rural counties (Ferry, Garfield) show the highest chronic disease and disability burdens, while King County (largest, most urban) shows the lowest.

2. **Yakima Challenges**: Yakima has the lowest adult percentage (71.37%) and struggles with high obesity (39.7%), diabetes (12.9%), and poor preventive care utilization.

3. **Prevention Paradox**: While cholesterol screening rates are high (83.77% average), high cholesterol remains the most prevalent condition (35.84%).

4. **Sleep Crisis**: Nearly 1 in 3 adults across all counties report short sleep duration (31.51%), representing a significant public health concern.

5. **Disability Burden**: Ferry County faces a severe disability crisis with 38.7% reporting any disability and 15.40% average across all disability measures.
