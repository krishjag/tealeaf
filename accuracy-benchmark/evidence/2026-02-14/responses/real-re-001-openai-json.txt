## A) Methodology (what to compute)

Let each record be a property \(i\).

### 1) Assessed value stats (assesstot)
- Total: \(\sum_i assesstot_i\)
- Average: \(\frac{1}{N}\sum_i assesstot_i\)
- Median: sort \(assesstot\); take middle (or average of two middles if even \(N\))
- Min/Max: \(\min_i assesstot_i\), \(\max_i assesstot_i\)

### 2) Building efficiency ratio
\[
eff_i = \frac{bldgarea_i}{lotarea_i}
\]
(If `lotarea` is 0/null, treat as undefined and exclude from ratio ranking.)

### 3) Assessed value per building sqft
\[
vpsf_i = \frac{assesstot_i}{bldgarea_i}
\]
(If `bldgarea` is 0/null, undefined; exclude.)

Rank top 5 and bottom 5 by `vpsf`.

### 4) Group by land use
Using your reference:
- 3 = Multi-Family Elevator
- 4 = Mixed Residential & Commercial
- 5 = Commercial & Office
- 8 = Public Facilities & Institutions
etc.

For each landuse group \(g\):
- count \(N_g\)
- avg assessed value: mean of `assesstot`
- avg building area: mean of `bldgarea`
- avg floors: mean of `numfloors`

### 5) FAR utilization %
Define zoning FAR cap proxy:
\[
farcap_i = \max(commfar_i, residfar_i, facilfar_i)
\]
Then:
\[
util_i = \frac{builtfar_i}{farcap_i}\times 100
\]
Notes:
- If all three caps are 0, utilization is undefined (common for PARK zoning / special cases).
- Values > 100% can happen due to data issues, bonuses, special permits, or mismatched cap proxy.

### 6) Total exempt value and exempt %
- Total exempt: \(\sum_i exempttot_i\)
- Exempt % of assessed: \(\frac{\sum_i exempttot_i}{\sum_i assesstot_i}\times 100\)
- Highest exemptions: rank by `exempttot`

### 7) Residential-unit metrics (unitsres > 0)
For each residential property:
- Res area per unit:
\[
rau_i = \frac{resarea_i}{unitsres_i}
\]
- Assessed value per res unit:
\[
avpu_i = \frac{assesstot_i}{unitsres_i}
\]

Compare across those properties (and flag outliers: very low `resarea/unit` often indicates missing/condo allocation quirks).
