## 6. Brand Owner Comparison: General Mills vs. Non-General Mills

### General Mills Products (19 products):
- **Average Calories:** 387 kcal
- **Average Sugar:** 21.9g
- **Average Fiber:** 6.5g
- **Average Protein:** 7.4g

### Non-General Mills Products (6 products):
- **Average Calories:** 444 kcal
- **Average Sugar:** 17.6g
- **Average Fiber:** 9.5g
- **Average Protein:** 11.2g

**Key Findings:**
- Non-GM products are more calorie-dense (+57 kcal)
- Non-GM products have LESS sugar (-4.3g) ✓
- Non-GM products have MORE fiber (+3.0g) ✓
- Non-GM products have significantly MORE protein (+3.8g) ✓

The non-General Mills products (particularly Dragons Blend and the biscuit products) offer better nutritional profiles with higher protein and fiber, despite higher calories.

## Summary Recommendations

**Best Overall Nutritional Profile:** Dragons Blend (Holy Crap brand)
- Highest protein (21.4g)
- Best sugar-to-fiber ratio (0.75)
- Zero sodium
- Highest fiber (14.3g)

**Best Mainstream Option:** Cheerios
- Low sugar (5.13g)
- High protein (12.8g)
- High fiber (10.3g)
- Excellent fortification

**Products to Approach with Caution:** 
- Boo Berry (worst sugar-to-fiber ratio)
- Rice Chex (highest sodium at 775mg)
- Cookie Crisp & Lucky Charms (highest sugar at 33.3g)
