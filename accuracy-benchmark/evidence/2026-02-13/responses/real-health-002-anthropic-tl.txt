### ðŸ” **Detailed Findings:**

**Health Outcomes (HLTHOUT):**
- High Cholesterol leads at 35.84%, followed closely by Obesity (33.44%) and High Blood Pressure (32.50%)
- Stroke is least prevalent at 3.38%

**Health Status (HLTHSTAT):**
- Fair/Poor General Health affects 18.30% on average
- Frequent Mental Distress (16.80%) exceeds Frequent Physical Distress (13.63%)

**Prevention (PREVENT):**
- Cholesterol Screening has excellent uptake (83.77%)
- Lack of Health Insurance is lowest at 9.06% (though Yakima spikes at 18.8%)

**Risk Behaviors (RISKBEH):**
- Short Sleep Duration is most prevalent risk behavior (31.51%)
- Physical Inactivity (19.82%) is second-highest concern
- Current Smoking (11.25%) is lowest among risk behaviors measured

**Disability (DISABLT):**
- Any Disability affects nearly 30% on average
- Cognitive Disability (14.41%) and Mobility Disability (12.75%) are most common specific types
- Self-care Disability is least common (3.56%)
