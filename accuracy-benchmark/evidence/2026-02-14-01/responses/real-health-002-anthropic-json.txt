## 6. Population-Weighted Obesity Prevalence

**Statewide Average (10 counties): 29.70%**

### County-Level Breakdown:
| Tier | Counties | Obesity Rate |
|------|----------|--------------|
| **Critical (35%+)** | Ferry (40.2%), Yakima (39.7%), Pierce (36.9%), Benton (36.2%), Clark (35.3%) | Above average |
| **Elevated (30-35%)** | Spokane (34.7%), Island (31.3%), Garfield (31.0%) | At/near average |
| **Lower (<30%)** | Whatcom (26.5%), King (22.6%) | Below average |

**Calculation Details:**
- Total adult population: 4,004,711
- Weighted sum: 118,944,320
- King County (largest population) has lowest rate, pulling state average down
- Rural Ferry & Yakima have rates **nearly 2x King County's**
