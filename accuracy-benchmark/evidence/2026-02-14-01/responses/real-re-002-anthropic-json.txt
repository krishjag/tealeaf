## Summary Insights

1. **Zoning**: Commercial C6 zones dominate (87%), enabling high-density development; manufacturing zones only 10%
2. **Age**: Modern towers (2000s-2010s) command 40% premium over older buildings, but 1930s landmarks remain highly valuable
3. **Use Mix**: Overwhelmingly single-use office (77%); only 3 properties truly mixed-use
4. **Floor Plates**: Inverse relationship between height and plate size; office towers average 42K sq ft/floor
5. **Ownership**: Highly fragmented; no dominant landlord, though Rockefeller Center controls 3 properties
6. **Site Coverage**: Midtown towers use 80-100% of lots; institutional campuses use <20%
7. **Geography**: Extreme concentration in Midtown 40.75-40.765 corridor; Hudson Yards emerging as new high-value cluster
