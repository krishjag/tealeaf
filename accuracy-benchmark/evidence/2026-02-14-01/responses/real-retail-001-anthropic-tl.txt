## 6. Brand Comparison: General Mills vs. Non-General Mills

### General Mills Products (19 products):
- **Average Calories:** 386 kcal/100g
- **Average Sugar:** 21.3 g/100g
- **Average Fiber:** 7.0 g/100g
- **Average Protein:** 7.5 g/100g

### Non-General Mills Products (6 products):
- **Average Calories:** 434 kcal/100g
- **Average Sugar:** 19.2 g/100g
- **Average Fiber:** 9.6 g/100g
- **Average Protein:** 10.8 g/100g

**Key Findings:**
- Non-GM products are more calorie-dense (+48 kcal)
- Non-GM products have slightly LESS sugar (-2.1g)
- Non-GM products have MORE fiber (+2.6g)
- Non-GM products have MORE protein (+3.3g)
