## 2. Sugar-to-Fiber Ratio Analysis

**Best (Lowest) Ratios:**
1. **Cheerios & Cheerios Cup: 0.50** ⭐ (Excellent - more fiber than sugar!)
2. Dragons Blend: 0.75
3. Bluey: 1.33
4. Rice Chex & Total: 1.50

**Worst (Highest) Ratios:**
1. **Boo Berry: 11.17** (11x more sugar than fiber)
2. Trix Mini: 6.75
3. Carmella Creeper: 7.24
4. Cookie Crisp & Lucky Charms: 5.95

**Key Finding:** Regular Cheerios products offer the best nutritional balance with half as much sugar as fiber.

## Summary Insights:

1. **Best Overall Nutrition:** Dragons Blend (highest protein, best sugar-fiber ratio after Cheerios, no sodium)
2. **Best Mainstream Cereal:** Cheerios (lowest sugar, highest fiber-to-sugar ratio, high protein)
3. **Most Fortified:** Total Cereal (100% DV for 10+ nutrients)
4. **Highest Sugar:** Cookie Crisp, Lucky Charms, Breakfast Pack (33.3g/100g)
5. **Watch Out For:** Boo Berry (sugar-to-fiber ratio of 11:1), Rice Chex (775mg sodium)
