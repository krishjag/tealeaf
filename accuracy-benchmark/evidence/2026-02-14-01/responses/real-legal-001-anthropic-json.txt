## **Case Outcome Summary**

This case ultimately resulted in the **conviction being reversed** by the Court of Appeals on April 25, 2024, with a remand to dismiss the complaint. After the State's petition for Supreme Court review, the Supreme Court issued its decision on June 27, 2025. The remittitur was filed September 5, 2025, and Attorney Lohr filed a Notice of Completion on October 2, 2025, concluding her representation after over 5 years on the case.
