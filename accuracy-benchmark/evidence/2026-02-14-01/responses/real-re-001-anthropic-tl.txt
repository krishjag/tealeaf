## Summary Findings

1. **One World Trade Center** (185 Greenwich) is the highest-assessed property at $1.67B
2. **One Vanderbilt** has the most intense development at 38x lot coverage
3. **Luxury residential condos** command $400-460/sqft, while affordable housing is valued at $67-90K per unit
4. **36% of total value** ($6.6B) is tax-exempt, primarily government/public facilities
5. **Air rights** are heavily utilized - 7 properties exceed their base zoning FAR by 150%+
6. **Commercial office** dominates (77% of properties), averaging 52 floors and $591M value
