## Summary Insights

1. **Concentration:** These 30 properties represent $18.3B in assessed value, dominated by Midtown office towers
2. **Development Intensity:** Modern office towers achieve 25-38× building-to-lot ratios through height
3. **Value Density:** Luxury mixed-use commands $350-460/sqft vs. $88-125/sqft for affordable housing
4. **Tax Policy:** Nearly $6B (32%) is tax-exempt through economic development programs and public ownership
5. **Zoning Evolution:** Many top properties exceed current FAR limits through grandfathering or air rights
6. **Housing Disparity:** 4 residential properties serve 17,113 units but with 50× value differences per unit
